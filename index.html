ABOUT US

MBSTUSC is a club of science.The members and the students of this club are interested in working on science related projects and they are also determined to do the needed works for this club. The club recently started its journey at the year 2019.the club already some amezing projects of its own already. The students of this club has also joined in many national compititions. Some of the members also has published their articles and which is of international class.


OBJECTIVES OF OUR CLUB:

A properly organised SCIENCE CLUB will be a valuable aid to teaching science and also means of motivating the students for learning science. Generally the main purpose is to develop interest in scientific hobbies and habits of exploration and creative faculties arranging science lectures, debates and seminers etc. this club make the students science minded and develop the phychomotor skills of them. And thats what our club is doing and on which we can firmly believe that it can add some great skills with our career!!!


ACTIVITIES OF OUR CLUB:

But in the very last days of the year 2019 it has changed its name. And the club is named then MBSTUSC (MBSTU SCIENCE CLUB). Here we are conjucted with almost all the science related subjects of our UNIVERSITY. We had taken many kinds of compititions and events in our university yet. We have also some plans now on national basis. We generally celebrate the scientific days of the year which are very important. We had a great ivent on the INTERNATIONAL SCIENCE DAY also.
